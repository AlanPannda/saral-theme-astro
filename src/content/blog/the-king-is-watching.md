# The King is Watching

A pixel-art roguelite kingdom builder where only the tiles under your Royal Gaze produce or train. You juggle economy vs. army while waves march along the road.

## Core Loop

**Place & arrange** farms, mines, workshops and barracks on the left grid; watch the enemy road on the right.

**Drive production with Gaze**: buildings only work when covered; expand/reshape your Gaze over time.

**Field your army**: trained units auto\-battle on the road; reconfigure base and Gaze between waves. 

**Events & modifiers** mix up each run;

**Meta progression** unlocks more options between attempts.
